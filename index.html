Better care, together

CareRing helps families coordinate caregiving — track medications, assign tasks, share updates, and stay connected. All in one free app.

💊

Medication Tracking

Track medications with dosages and schedules. Get reminders, log doses, and see the full history. Generate care reports for doctor visits.

📋

Task Coordination

Create tasks, set priorities, assign to family members. Everyone sees what needs to be done and what's already covered.

💬

Daily Check-Ins

Record mood, pain, sleep, appetite, and mobility. Spot trends and keep remote family members in the loop.

Shared Care Notes

Keep a shared journal of observations and important details. Pin critical notes so they're always visible to the team.

Medical Info Hub

Emergency contacts, conditions, and allergies in one secure place. Accessible to every circle member when it matters.
